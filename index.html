About Us

At Trek Mental Health, we are committed to advancing mental health care through innovative technology solutions. With a deep-rooted history in collaboration with leading institutions like the National Institutes of Health (NIH) and the Department of Defense (DoD), we leverage cutting-edge research and data-driven insights to create scalable, effective mental health tools.

Our team blends expertise in clinical research and technology to develop AI-driven tools that improve mental health care capabilities. We empower individuals and care partners to promote healthier lives.
